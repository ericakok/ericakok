        My name's Erica, and I'm from the Philippines. I am currently a Computer Science and Finance double major at Stetson University in DeLand, Florida, and in just four short years, I will be walking across the stage, along with the entire class of 2019 all over the world, with these two degrees, and perhaps even a few minors, attached to my name. I'm certain that time will indeed fly by, and so I want to make sure that I manage to make the most out of these few years in college. I've heard over and over again how these years are supposed to be the most exciting and exhilirating time of our lives. During these four years, I am apparently supposed to learn life- long lessons that I won't be able to get from anywhere else, and honestly, whether these may be true or not, I am so excited for this new journey to begin. I want to learn as much as I can, and I want to further explore my strengths and my capabilities. I hope to use this site as a tool to aid me in doing so. Through the creation of this site, I hope to showcase my abilities and thus earn opportunities such as acceptance into Hack-a-thons, internships, and perhaps even positions as a full-time employee. So, here's to taking chances, to several opportunities that lie ahead, to learning and growing, and of course, to the start of a brand new chapter in my life May these next four years be filled with new experiences, new challenges, new memories, and a new sense of fulfillment, contentment, and joy.
